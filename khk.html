Kids Helping Kids

Kids Helping Kids is a non-profit organization in Stamford, CT that inspires leadership through youth led service projects. While working with answerYes consulting, I contributed to the redesign and development of their new website, building out all of the pages in WordPress, writing a custom child theme, and providing them with a style guide to use as they continue to maintain their site.

Process

Before starting the redesign, I did an invetory of KHK's current site at the time and made two site maps to reflect the current information architecture of the site and the proposed information architecture for the redesign.





Throughout the course of the project, I communicated with my team and with our client, KHK, using Basecamp. We used the message board to communicate about ongoing questions and concerns and to document our meeting notes. We also used the To-dos to manage our tasks.


Color Palette

The color pallete we chose to use reflected the colors in the KHK logo as well as a couple of complimentary accent colors. We used the accent colors to create banners for their newsletter sign up and new project creation.
